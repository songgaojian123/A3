Fitts Law Study: Before Sleep VS Just Woke up

Assignment 3

Authors: Gaojian Song

Summary:

For this Fitts Law Study, I tested 2 people before their sleep time, and when they just woke up to see the different, on a simple mouse task. I tested three users before their sleep time, and when they just woke up to check the ablity of move a mouse and click different objects of varying widths and amplitudes. Each test subject did 8 sessions of 25 clicks. The users first did the task before their sleep time and then when they just woke up. Then compared the throughput grand mean for each condition.
